CV


My name is Zixuan (Steve) Feng. I am pursuing Ph.D., advised by Anita Sarma in the Department of Electrical Engineering Computer Science and Statistics at Oregon State University (OSU). My research explores the human factors in software engineering by drawing on user experience (UX), human-computer interaction (HCI), empirical software engineering, social computing, and computer-supported cooperative work (CSCW). I collect and analyze data from open-source software (OSS) projects using statistical analysis, grounded theory, and large language models (LLMs) to develop or validate theories about software engineering processes.
